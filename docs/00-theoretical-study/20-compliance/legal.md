# Legal & Regulatory Compliance

## 1. Data Privacy
*   **GDPR/Local Law**: Implementation of data minimization and "Right to Access/Delete".
*   **Consent Management**: Clear opt-in/opt-out for marketing and tracking.

## 2. Financial Regulations
*   **AML (Anti-Money Laundering)**: Monitoring for suspicious transaction patterns.
*   **KYC (Know Your Customer)**: Mandatory verification for high-volume merchants.

## 3. Platform Policies
*   **Terms of Service (TOS)**: Clear rules for sellers and buyers.
*   **Dispute Resolution**: Legal framework for handling refunds and fraud claims.
