# Interaction & Social Engagement

## 1. Gamification & Reactions
*   Floating hearts/likes (M3 style).
*   Top viewer badges.
*   "Flash Sale" countdowns triggered by the seller.

## 2. Moderation
*   **Automatic**: Filter for profanity and spam links.
*   **Manual**: Seller/Staff can kick or mute viewers.
*   **Admin-level**: Global moderators can shutdown streams violating TOS.

## 3. Analytics (Post-Stream)
*   Total viewers.
*   Peak concurrency.
*   Conversion rate (Sales vs Viewers).
*   Engagement map (When did people drop off?).
