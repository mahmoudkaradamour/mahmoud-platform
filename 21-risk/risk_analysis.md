# Risk Analysis & Mitigation

## 1. Technical Risks
*   **Single Point of Failure**: Mitigation via multi-region deployment and redundant database replicas.
*   **Scaling Bottleneck**: Mitigation via early load testing and auto-scaling groups.

## 2. Business Risks
*   **Merchant Fraud**: Mitigation via manual vetting and automated escrow releases.
*   **Market Volatility**: Mitigation via multi-currency support and real-time exchange rate updates.

## 3. Operational Risks
*   **Staff Access**: Mitigation via strict RBAC and mandatory MFA for all internal tools.
*   **Data Breach**: Mitigation via proactive pentesting and field-level encryption.
